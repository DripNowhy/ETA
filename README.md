# ETA: Evaluating Then Aligning Safety of Vision Language Models at Inference-Time

</div>

This paper focus on inference-time safety alignment of Vision Language Models (VLMs), which decomposes the alignment process into two phase: i) Evaluating input visual contents and output responses to establish a robust safety awareness in multimodal settings, and ii) Aligning unsafe behaviors at both shallow and deep levels by conditioning the VLMsâ€™ generative distribution with an interference prefix and performing sentence-level best-of-N to search the most harmless and helpful generation paths.
